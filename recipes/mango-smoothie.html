Mango Smoothie

Description

This is my 'health-conscious' mango smoothie recipe. Because it features many types of superseeds and is dairy free. Though you can opt out or swap any of the ingredients as per your preference. Remember, baby steps to healthy eating!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mango
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 tbsp flaxseeds
  • 2 tbsp hempseeds
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/8 cup hempseed oil
  • 1/2 cup protein powder
  • 1/2 cup probiotic yoghurt
  • 2/3 cup almond milk
