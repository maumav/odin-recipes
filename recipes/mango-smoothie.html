Mango Smoothie

Description

This is my 'health-conscious' mango smoothie recipe. Because it features many types of superseeds and is dairy free. Though you can opt out or swap any of the ingredients as per your preference. Remember, baby steps to healthy eating!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mango
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 tbsp flaxseeds
  • 2 tbsp hempseeds
  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/8 cup hempseed oil
  • 1/2 cup protein powder
  • 1/2 cup probiotic yoghurt
  • 2/3 cup almond milk

Steps

  1. Grab 1 cup frozen mango. Chuck it straigt in the blender. There's no need to defrost as its convenient instead adding in ice.
  2. Grab 1 ripe banana and break it in half. Chuck into blender.
  3. Add in your 2 tbsp flaxseeds, hempseeds and chia seeds into the blender.
  4. Add the 1/8 cup hempseed oil.
  5. Add 1/2 cup protein powder.
  6. Add 1/2 cup probiotic yoghurt and 2/3 cup almond milk.
  7. Then, blend till smooth to your liking!
  8. Finally, pour smoothie into your favourite glass and enjoy!
