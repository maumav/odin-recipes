Easy Tuna-Egg Breakfast

Description

This is an easy tuna and egg sandwich for breakfast recipe. Pack with protein to start your day easily with a full stomach.

Ingredients

  • 1 5 ounce can of tuna
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 whole onion, chopped
  • A sunflower oil for cooking
  • 1 handful of baby spinach, minced
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • 4 sliced rye bread

Steps

  1. Drain the can of tuna and place in a mixing bowl.
  2. Set stove to medium-low heat. Place a frying pan and drizzle with sunflower oil. Wait to heat up.
  3. Peel, rinse and chop the whole onion. Set aside
  4. Grab the handful of baby spinach and mince. Add into the mixing bowl.
  5. Check pan. If the oil is deemed ready, place chopped onion and cook till its lightly brown.
  6. Crack the 2 eggs and add into the mixing bowl.
  7. Mix the ingredients in the bowl well and add a pinch of salt and pepper.
