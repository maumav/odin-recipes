Zucchini and Parmesan Cheese Fritters

Description

Make an easy and simple bite-sized snack made of Zucchini and Parmesan Cheese!

Ingredients

  • 3 large zucchini, finely grated
  • A pinch of salt

Batter

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Ground pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Toppings
