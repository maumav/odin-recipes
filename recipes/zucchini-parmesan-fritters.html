Zucchini and Parmesan Cheese Fritters

Description

Make an easy and simple bite-sized snack made of Zucchini and Parmesan Cheese!

Ingredients

  • 3 large zucchini, finely grated
  • A pinch of salt

Batter

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Ground pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Toppings

  • 1 tbsp Parmesan cheese, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Combine grated zucchini and a pinch of salt into a bowl and stir. Set aside for 10 minutes.
    Pour mixture into a clean dish towel or cheese cloth and squeeze to drain thouroughly.
  2. Whisk egg in a bowl and add flour, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper.
    Stir in drained zucchini and mix well.
  3. Heat oil in a medium-sized pan in medium-high heat.Add batter by the tablespoon.
    Cook fritters until golden brown on both sides, about 5 minutes per batch.
    Transfer to serving plate and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and salt.
    Serve immediately with sour cream.
